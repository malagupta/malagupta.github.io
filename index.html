About

Bridging Java & Community

Mala works as a Developer Advocate at JetBrains. Founder and lead mentor at eJavaGuru.com, she has been coaching students and professionals to succeed in Java certification since 2006. A sought-after speaker at conferences like Oracle Code One, QCon, DevNexus, and JBCN, she holds over 20 years of experience as an author, mentor, consultant, technology leader and developer. She co-leads the Delhi Java User Group and co-founded KaagZevar.com.
